About Jaipur

Jaipur is the capital city of Rajasthan, India. It is famous for its rich culture, beautiful palaces, historic forts, and vibrant markets. Jaipur was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II.

Famous Places in Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

The Palace of Winds is one of the most famous landmarks of Jaipur.

Amber Fort

A beautiful fort located on a hilltop, known for its artistic architecture.

City Palace

A royal residence showcasing Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.
